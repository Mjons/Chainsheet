What pain points is CoinSheet addressing?

Costly typos

Manually inputing pricing and trading data one value at a time is not only tedious and time consuming but is innately prone to typos that can cost you money and sanity down the line.

Confusing pricing

The pricing of assets can vary significantly based on local demand and regulations. This complicates things when you are trying to find accurate data for your use case.

Dispersed assets

It’s invaluable to have access to a bird’s eye view of your investments. However, keeping track of your portfolio is difficult when your assets are spread across various wallets and exchanges.

Why use CoinSheet?

Cryptocurrency is now one of the top investment options available to corporations, businesses and individuals looking to diversify their portfolio. Yet there are no secure and reliable tools to help manage this type of asset efficiently. CoinSheet was created to address this gap through the use of APIs that pull the data you need straight from the sources you trust.
